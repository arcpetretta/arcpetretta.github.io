  • Updating the site!

    • If you have been following me through phase 0 you will have noticed that I have made a lot of aesthetic improvements to the site this past week. A lot of it was just toying around with CSS and making things a little prettier in general.

    It's funny because the difference between my sight from a visual standpoint is almost night and day. Yet, changing backgrounds and editing the properties of different objects really isn't all that complicated. If I looked at this sight 9 weeks ago I'd tell you there's no way I could make it despite it being pretty simple. It's amazing what dedication and practice will do even in short amounts of time.

    I was given one suggestion by a peer to make my "about me" and "resume" boxes closer together on the homepage. Personally, I liked them the way they were before but I went ahead and changed their orientation to please the masses (I'm a man of the people!). I'm not sure if moving things around makes that big of a difference. However, as my site becomes more and more advanced it may eventually contribute to the overall user experience (UX).

    In any regard, I'm off to phase 1, people. Wish me luck and hopefully I'll have time to continue making updates. Keep on keepin' on.
